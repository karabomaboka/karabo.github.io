History

Gaba Primary School

The school has started in 1918 at the headman's kraal accommondated at the headman's hall called Tshivhamboni.It Later on relocated to Dzamba, Gudu a "sub-village of Gaba". The first educator was Mr Nemabidi Nthabalala. The school then relocated again to Tshikhudini Mutulumeni. Another teacher called Mr Ramashia was appointed,that made the school to have two educators.It then relocated again to Salvation Army church; where Mr Mhlanga and Mr Tshivhi were appointed as new teachers,then Mr Marema was appointed in 1966 - 1968. In 1969, Mr Tshibubudze became the first principal. He was working with Mrs Tshivhase Agnes and Mr Makhadi Piet as his assistants.

In 1981, Mr Tshibubudze left and Mr Nemakonde R.E was appointed in his position.In 1986,Mr Nemakonde left and Mr Netshivhazwaulu T.S was appointed in his position. There after two other schools emerged at Gaba Village, a secondary school, George Ntodeni and another primary school, Thomani Primary School.
